Our Story

Howard’s Lakeside General Store is a family-owned bait and tackle shop in Springfield, Ohio. We serve our community with live bait, tackle, camping supplies, and friendly outdoor advice.

We’re passionate about fishing and helping every customer get ready for a great day by the lake.

Reel Respooling Service

Bring your reels in and let us handle the hard part! We’ll replace your old line and respool with the type and weight you prefer. Quick 24-hour turnaround guaranteed.

  • Monofilament, braided, or fluorocarbon options
  • Choose your preferred line weight
  • Affordable flat rate per reel
